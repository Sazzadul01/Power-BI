I’m excited to share my latest Power BI project – a Mobile Sales Dashboard designed to analyze and visualize mobile sales performance in a clear and interactive way.

Dashboard Features:
Total Sales by City (Map Visualization)
Total Quantity by Month (Trend Analysis)
Total Sales by Mobile Model
Brand-wise Performance (Sales, Quantity, Transactions)
Transactions by Payment Method (Cash, Card, bKash, Nagad, Rocket)
Customer Ratings Distribution
Sales by Day Name
Monthly Slicer for Dynamic Filtering

Others: Data Modeling, DAX Calculations, Data Visualization, Interactive Reporting, Business Insight Generation

This dashboard helps in:
Identifying top-performing brands and models
Understanding monthly sales trends
Analyzing customer buying behavior
Tracking payment method preferences
Making data-driven business decisions
